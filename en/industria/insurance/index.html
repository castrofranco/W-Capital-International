Insurance

Our firm represents insurance companies, reinsurers, project developers and contractors in all matters related to insurance, including providing advice on regulatory matters and changes, and devising insurance strategies for specific projects working closely with insurance brokers.

We have actively participated in M&A transactions in the insurance and reinsurance businesses and the design of insurance-backed project finance. Our insurance and litigation teams have also the experience and skills to represent clients in their insurance claims, in mediation processes between insurers and beneficiaries, and in proceedings in judicial and administrative venues involving insurance companies, reinsurers, insurance brokers and claims adjusters.
