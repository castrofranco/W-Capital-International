Healthcare

Our experience in various industries, as well as being one of the largest full-service law firms in Chile, allow us to provide a multidisciplinary service to our clients in accordance with the unique changes and challenges that the healthcare industry faces these days, including the increased government regulations, technological innovations and the strengthened customer demands.

We advise companies in the healthcare industry on regulatory matters, competition issues, strategic technology licensing, collaboration agreements, intellectual property and sales or distribution strategies and transactions.

In the healthcare infrastructure sector, we regularly advise on development, planning and construction, environmental support, private/public partnerships, employment issues, tax issues, commercial agreements, M&A and restructuring.

We act for companies and their investors across the pharmaceutical, cosmetical, food, biotech and medtech industries. We have advised on issues including health claims, composition and labelling requirements, data protection, marketing issues and a variety of support on litigation and regulatory risks.

We have advised domestic and foreign pharmaceutical chains and laboratories in asset acquisition transactions and in the marketing and distribution of medicines and food products in Chile.
