Transportation

Our firm’s experience in the transportation field is broad, and covers all kinds of matters and transactions involving the shipping, airline and land freight businesses. Our lawyers have an unmatched record and knowledge in transportation sector, being active players in the development of Chile’s modernization of maritime and air transport regulation during the last few decades.

We have participated, marshalling the resources of our M&A, finance, insurance, antitrust and litigation teams, in complex asset finance transactions for vessels and aircraft (purchase, leasing, loan), in the financing of railroad and subway networks, in the financing and acquisition of assets for the public transportation network of Santiago, in the largest merger transactions in the airline business and cross-border joint ventures in the shipping industry, and in highly sophisticated antitrust cases and inquiries regarding concentration operations and business arrangements in the commercial air transportation sector.
