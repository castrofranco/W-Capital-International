Real Estate

Our firm advises on all aspects of real estate, from due diligence, structuring and negotiation to the drafting and completion of the most complex deals, including partnering with private and institutional investors, developers, owners, and lenders in real estate joint ventures, financings, development projects, leasing, and acquisitions and dispositions.

Our title review skills in real estate transactions are recognized in the market for their completeness, precision and business sense, combining the background and knowledge of our different areas to provide a global and efficient view of the asset’s ownership, construction, use and permits.

Our clients rely on our deep experience for their investments in a wide range of real estate-related businesses and asset classes, which have included industrial, office, retail, multi-family, hotel, senior housing and agricultural properties.
