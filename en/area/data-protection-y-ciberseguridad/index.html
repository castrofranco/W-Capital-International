Data Protection and Cybersecurity

Our firm advises clients in diverse industries on the privacy, data protection and cybersecurity challenges impacting their businesses, helping them to manage their obligations and legal risks, while getting the most value from their information. Our team is constantly tracking changes in privacy laws and enforcement practices that may affect our clients.

Our services comprise strategic advice on compliance and data governance inside and outside the company, e-commerce and social media, including issues related to website notices; privacy and web-tracking policies; usage of data in marketing; online payments; cloud computing; privacy impact assessments; security breaches and cybersecurity preparedness-incident response. We also assist our clients on IT Agreements, data transfer and management agreements as also in the assessment and management of data privacy risks in corporate and financial transactions, including due diligence in M&A transactions.
