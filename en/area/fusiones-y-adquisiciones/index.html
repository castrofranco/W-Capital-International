Mergers and Acquisitions

Our firm advises on all aspects of M&A, from due diligence, structuring and negotiation to the drafting and completion of the most complex acquisition agreements, including share and asset deals, cross-border mergers, joint venture arrangements, takeovers and exchange offers of dual listed companies.

Our firm is a market leader in M&A work and clients engage our services because of our proven expertise in public and private tender offers, securities laws and corporate governance issues inherent to such transactions. We further put at the service of the client all our resources from our tax, environmental, labor, intellectual property, real estate and litigation teams to assess and help mitigate all risks related to such transactions, and ensure they come to fruition in the best possible outcome for the client’s interests. As such, we have participated in most of the landmark transactions in the Chilean market over the past three decades.

We have represented in these types of transactions sellers, purchasers, majority and minority shareholders, the target company, whether public or private, and financiers in a wide range of operations, including mergers, splits and spin-offs, business combinations, public and private tender offers, corporate reorganizations or other kinds of restructuring. We also offer our clients support in their search for strategic partners, venture capital, or other types of business prospects.
