Labor and Employment

Our firm advises clients on a wide range of matters that concern employers and their relationship with employees, including the negotiation and drafting of agreements, as well as conflict resolution and litigation that arises during the employment relationship and as a consequence of its termination.

Our labor team is renowned for handling a complete array of matters, from general labor and social security issues, planning and strategy, to the labor aspects of corporate restructuring and M&A transactions, as well as collective bargaining and negotiations with unions and governmental authorities. Further, we have the capacity and experience necessary to represent our clients in all kinds of trials, including for subsidiary liability and work accidents, before the specialized labor tribunals and the higher courts.

We also provide advice to our clients and their foreign employees and service providers in their immigration requirements, assisting them during the process to obtain visas, and work and residence permits.
