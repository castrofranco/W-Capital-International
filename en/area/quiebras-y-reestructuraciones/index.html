Reorganization and Insolvency

Our reorganization and insolvency team is highly qualified to provide integral advice to our clients, responding in an efficient manner to the legal challenges that a situation of insolvency may result to creditors and debtors in different areas and industries.

When companies undergo difficulties that threaten their solvency, we advise our clients on the reorganization and restructuring of their liabilities, in the negotiation of payment plans and other workouts with their creditors. Likewise, we represent the interests of different creditors, including liquidation proceedings and reorganization agreements.

Our reorganization and insolvency team also works closely with the other areas of the firm to provide an integrated service, proposing and implementing safeguards in all kinds of agreements and arrangements to protect the rights and interests of our clients in anticipation of potential counterparty insolvency risks.
