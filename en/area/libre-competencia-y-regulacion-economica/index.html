Antitrust and Economic Regulation

Our firm has a widely recognized antitrust practice, being constantly named among the leading firms and individuals in the field. We have participated in some of the most relevant merger control procedures and antitrust litigation of the last decade in Chile.

Our antitrust team keeps always up to date with the most recent trends in the U.S. and EU antitrust regulations and cases, applying the latest theories and arguments in the defense of its clients.

We are used to providing preventive advice to clients, setting up internal control and risk management manuals and processes, conducting talks and making presentations to management and compliance teams, adopting measures to act in accordance with applicable laws, and reviewing proposed or potential agreements and business arrangements.

We have represented companies in a broad range of sectors, including industry-wide business associations, in merger control procedures and industry inquiries before the Chilean Antitrust Prosecutor, and in litigation of antitrust matters before the Chilean Antitrust Court and the ordinary courts.

Our antitrust team has unmatched skills and experience in the market to offer clients the fullest extent of services to navigate through all stages of any matter, proposal or venture with competition implications.
