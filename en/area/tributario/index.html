Tax

Our tax team is well known for giving sound tax advice in complex domestic and cross-border transactions. Multinational and large local clients are frequently reaching out to us to find innovative solutions to their requirements and/or needs.  In this sense, we assist our clients in structuring their businesses in a tax-efficient manner in accordance with the applicable legal framework.

We have a recognized capacity to correctly assess risks relating to tax matters, so our clients can be confident that when taking a business decision they have been provided with all of the information they need to do so. Whether in writing or verbally, our recommendations to clients are always given after a thorough and well-founded analysis.

Our tax team has also a solid background in corporate law and litigation, which allows us to provide our clients with a comprehensive service that includes not only legal advice from a tax regulatory perspective, but also in M&A, capital markets, project finance, and in the increasing number of tax cases before the recently-created Tax Courts.

In addition to the design and implementation of tax efficient schemes, our work includes the analysis –mostly provided in the form of a legal opinion or memorandum– of the tax implications of a specific transaction or business model that our clients may have or are planning to carry out, as well as the review of the tax situation of companies within their business groups
