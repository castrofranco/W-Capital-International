Capital Markets

Our experience in the securities markets is significant both in Chile and abroad. Given the complexity and diversity of the transactions in which we have participated, we can assure our clients that we are intimately familiar with the rules that govern this area of the law, as well as the practices that apply in foreign markets.

In this area, along with our banking and finance practice, innovation and creativity in the development of new products has earned us special recognition for years.

Our capital markets team has been recognized for the design and structuring of sophisticated securities transactions comprising multilateral financing and cross-border capital flows, as well as for the development of new products. Investment banks and issuers consider our capital markets team to be their first choice for domestic and international securities transactions that require cross-practice knowledge, skills, experience and commercial approach.

Our lawyers regularly represent key parties in all types of significant local and international of securities transactions, including public and private offerings such as IPOs, equity and investment-grade and high-yield debt offerings, project bonds, securities exchange listings, exchange offers, cash and debt tender offers and hybrid securities. Our clients include issuers, underwriters, initial purchasers, institutional and individual investors, selling shareholders and those providing credit support through guarantees, surety bonds and letters of credit.
