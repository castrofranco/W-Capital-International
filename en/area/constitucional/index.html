Constitutional

Constitutional law has acquired increasing relevance in the actions of individuals and groups, in the sense that its direct application to substantive relationships has been recognized, and the effective protection of constitutional guaranties has been especially regulated, as is the case with motions for protective orders and the special protective action in cases of discrimination by the State in economic matters.

On the other hand, after the 2005 amendment to the Chilean Constitution, the function of the Constitutional Court in declaring legal norms inapplicable on grounds of unconstitutionality whether with particular or general effects, has transformed its role into a fundamental tool for the defense of a person’s interests. With such an understanding, our firm renders services to its clients in a wide array of constitutional law matters, including before the ordinary Courts and the Constitutional Court.
